ABOUT US

Super Saiyan Rosé (Sūpā Saiya-jin Roze) is the Super Saiyan God Super Saiyan (Sūpā Saiya-jin Goddo Sūpā Saiya-jin) transformation assumed by Saiyans who possess proper god-like status.

It is Goku Black's version of the Super Saiyan Blue form and matches it in strength. This transformation puts Goku Black in his own class all together.

POPULAR INFO

The Super Saiyan Rosé form was created by author Akira Toriyama uniquely for Goku Black, though he simply referred to it as a "Super Saiyan of a slightly different color than Goku."

Due to being the same form, this state is identical to Super Saiyan Blue in all but color. The hair is light pink in color with pure-white highlights, the eyebrows are light pink, and the eyes turn light gray although they appear light pink through the form's aura.
